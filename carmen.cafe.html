Carmen's

Description

Before the Food Festival

Carmen's is a petite little cafe, almost hidden away between its neighbors. There are very little customers and it doesn't even have a sign until Level 6. The only employee shown is Carmen.

After the Food Festival

The number of customers has rapidly increased following Carmen's win. Helena now works there as well, and the game shows an upstairs balcony.
