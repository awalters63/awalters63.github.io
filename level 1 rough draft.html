Level 1 - Arrival

Pre-Unpacking:

Maria is on the train to Bellariva, excitedly looking forward to the chance to take a break from her year of traveling and restoring antiques. Before she can explore, however, she needs to unpack her suitcase.

Unpacking - Aventura Classic: Suitcase

This level takes place on the tiles of the hotel floor. The coffee cup in the corner leads to the menu.

  1. Remove the toolbox and set it to the side. (After this, the order of steps is up to the player.)
  2. Remove the overalls and set them to the side.
  3. Remove the sunglasses and set them to the side.
  4. Remove the hairbrush and set it to the side.
  5. Remove the earrings, one at a time, and set them to the side.
  6. Remove the passport and set it to the side.
  7. Remove the picture and set it to the side.
  8. Remove the camera and set it to the side.
  9. Remove the flyers and set them to the side.

Post-Unpacking:

Maria is in Bellariva to visit their food festival, however she will need to do jobs in order to pay for it. After she is settled, she will pass out her flyers in order to find work. The player learns that Maria's parents owned an antique shop, and that is where she learned to make repairs and gained her love for antiques.
