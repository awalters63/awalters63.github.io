Bellariva

The town of Bellariva is a quiet, picturesque, seemingly perfect town. Over the course of the game, the weather is nothing but beautiful, constantly warm and sunny. Tourists are in town for the Food Fsetival, leading to busier-than-usual cafes and restaurants.
