Level 9 - The Bigger Picture

Pre-Restoration

It's Maria's last job of the day, at Carmen's apartment. Helena opens the door and makes a remark on how dirty the apartment is. She's cleaning it out in order to find items to sell at the market during the festival, in order to help with Carmen's money struggles. She pulls out a broken slide projector, previously owned by their parents.

Restoration - Omnicolor: Slide Projector

This level takes place on the tiles of Carmen's apartment in the shadows of plants. The coffee cup in the corner leads to the menu.

  1. Unscrew and disassemble the slide projector.
  2. Replace the broken lightbulb.
  3. Add two mirrors and turn them until the light is reflected upwards.
  4. Put the top back on and add the final mirror, rotating until a picture appears.
  5. Add the button and attach the wire.
  6. Put the cover back on and screw everything in.
  7. Put the new slides into the slide deck and flip through each slide.

Post-Restoration

Helena tells Maria that she feels like she's let Carmen down and that she's lost her job, leaving her unable to help with Carmen's money troubles. She wanted to tell Carmen, but felt that all Carmen cared about was herself. But with all that Helena has lost, she doesn't want to lose Carmen too.
