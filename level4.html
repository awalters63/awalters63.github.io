Level 4 - Disconnected

Pre-Restoration:

It's Maria's second day in Bellariva and she wanders past busy cafes. Carmen, owner of Carmen's, calls her over and asks her to fix her phone so that she can call her sister in preparation of the food festival.

Restoration - Everdial: Rotary Phone

This level takes place on the stones of Carmen's. The coffee cup in the corner leads to the menu.

  1. Unscrew the screws on the back and remove the casing.
  2. Unscrew the broken bell and remove it, before replacing it and screwing it back in.
  3. Unscrew the dial and remove the wire.
  4. Replace the circuit board and connect the wires.
  5. Screw the dial back in, as well as the casing.

Post-Restoration:

Maria gives Carmen the phone back, but she doesn't seem enthused to call her sister. Carmen muses about her bills and rents before telling Maria her coffee is on the house. She calls her sister and it becomes clear that she needs to borrow money from her. However, she doesn't want to see her and, when her sister decides to visit, begins to stress. She asks Maria to come by tomorrow.
