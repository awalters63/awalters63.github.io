Level 6 - In the Dark

Pre-Assembly:

Maria returns to Carmen's and finds Carmen frantically cleaning. She tells Maria that she is scared of Helena finding the cafe a mess, and Maria can tell how stressed she is. Carmen brings Maria to a back room and asks her to help her assemble a neon sign.

Assembly - Carmen's: Neon Sign

This level takes place in the shadows of a window and blinds. The coffee cup in the corner leads to the menu.

  1. Attach the LED parts to the sign.
  2. Unscrew the back of the sign and remove it.
  3. Apply glue and attach the button.
  4. Attach a wire from the button to the circuit board.
  5. Press the power button.
  6. Glue on the new circuit strip and add another wire, adjusting them so that they are all connected.
  7. Press the power button.
  8. Glue on the new circuit strip and add another wire, adjusting them so that they are all connected.
  9. Pick an icon to place on the front. Although there are three icons to choose from, the game defaults to the ice cream in other images.
  10. Press the power button.
  11. Replace the back of the sign and screw it back in.

Post-Assembly:

Maria and Carmen mount the sign and Carmen reflects on when she first opened the cafe. Helena arrives and comments on how the cafe isn't open, putting Carmen down again. She moves inside the cafe and says that the first thing they'll be doing is taking down the sign.
