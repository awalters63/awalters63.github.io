Level 14 (Epilogue) - Carmen's Letter

Pre-Assembly

This is the only level where Maria is not the main character. Instead, Carmen is writing a letter to Maria, telling her what she's missed in the time sense leaving. Her cafe has been manic and Helena has been helping her manage it. But although Helena has adapted well, Carmen can tell she misses the city and wants to make her a fancy coffee with milk art, like she used to get. She builds her new coffee machine all by herself.

Assembly - Newcombe Classic: Expresso Machine

This level takes place on a wooden backdrop. Rather than a coffee cup, a cookie in the corner leads to the menu.

  1. Unscrew the top and connect the piping, shown in the picture.
  2. Add the machine parts and connect the wiring, matching the icons.
  3. Unscrew the bottom of the machine and add the circuit board in.
  4. Connect the wiring, matching the one dot icon to the bolt icon, the two dots icon to the rays icon, and the three dots icon to the cup icon.
  5. Unscrew the front of the machine and connect the wiring.
  6. Replace all parts and screws.
  7. Press the buttons to test them, brew a coffee, and add the milk ark. Once the player is finished with their design, click once to finalize it.

Post-Assembly

Carmen wasn't the proudest of her coffee, but Helena enjoyed it. She spent a long time smelling the coffee, and was impressed by the art, even though she couldn't figure out what it was. She asked for another one, which means Carmen has to practice now. Carmen thanks Maria for all of her help, realizing how hard the repairs she made for her were.
