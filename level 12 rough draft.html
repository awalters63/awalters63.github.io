Level 12 - A Father's Reprise

Pre-Restoration

It's the final morning Maria is in Bellariva. She gets a call to Joseph' and Izzy's House, where she finds Joseph tidying the garden and Izzy practicing with a screwdriver. Joseph tells Maria that Izzy wants to be a repair woman, just like her. They move to Joseph's office, where he shows Maria a music box that stopped working.

Restoration - Portofino "Dove Nest": Music Box

This level takes place in the shadow of a window. The coffee cup in the corner leads to the main menu.

  1. Open the music box and unscrew the picture.
  2. Apply glue and add the new picture, screwing the rim back on.
  3. Remove the cover and the drawer, and unscrew the gear piece.
  4. Take apart the gears and align correctly. Place the gear piece back in place.
  5. Remove the dove from the drawer, and put the cover and the dove into their correct places.
  6. Replace the base and put the screws back, as well as the drawer.
  7. Insert the key.

Post-Restoration

Joseph laments on what he has missed and expresses how he wants to live up the person his wife would want him to be. He regrets the way he has becoem his father, but from now on Izzy comes first. Izzy runs in and they reconcile. Joseph thanks Maria for what she has done for them. Maria knows that she has something she needs to fix for herself.
