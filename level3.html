Level 3 - Shattered

Pre-Restoration:

After repairing Izzy's cassette deck, Maria did a couple more small jobs around town. When she returns to her hotel, there is a message waiting for her, asking her to visit Jospeh' and Izzy's house. Here, she meets Joseph, the mayor of Bellariva, who wants to pay her for fixing Izzy's cassette deck. Maria notices a statue, formerly belonging to Joseph's father, that had been broken by Izzy, which Joseph asks her to fix.

Restoration - Wentwood Porcelain: Statue

This level takes place under the shadows of a window and plants. The coffee cup in the corner leads to the menu.

  1. Apply glue to the base and add the part with a leg and the cape.
  2. Reapply glue and add the part with the other leg.
  3. Reapply glue and add the chest part.
  4. Reapply glue and add the part with the arm carrying a book.
  5. Reapply glue and add the other arm.
  6. Reapply glue and add the head.

Post-Repair:

Joseph talks about his relationship with his father and sets the statue down next to a family portrait of him, his wife, and Izzy. He tells Maria that he has been working late in preparation for the festival, which prompts her to remember how her parents would find ways to be busy so they wouldn't have to talk about her leaving. She hopes that in addition to his work, Joseph is making time for Izzy.
