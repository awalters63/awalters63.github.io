Joseph' & Izzy's House

Description

Joseph and Izzy live in a quaint little seafront cottage. It's a beautiful house even with its flaking paintwork and chipped tiles. After Joseph and Izzy reconcile, there is a noticeable transformation where the house feels more alive.

Garden & Playhouse

Before the festival, the garden is beautiful and unruly, overflowing with colorful flowers. The wooden playhouse is crawling with vines and the entrance is blocked by a large rosebush. Inside, the floor is worn and there is a blue table that Izzy has doodled on. After the festival, Joseph cleans the playhouse and the garden, letting light into the house.

Office

Joseph's office is an orderly room filled with papers and ledgers. This is where Joseph keeps his father's statue and the picture of his family.
