Carmen

Physical Appearance

Carmen is the owner of the cafe Carmen's. She has dark brown hair, pulled back with a blue scrunchie, and brown eyes. She wears a yellow shirt and light blue pants with a blue towel over one shoulder and a blue apron. She has gold hoop earrings.

Personality

Carmen is a very friendly person, though she stresses easily. She is a bit chaotic and can forget things, including items she has collected in her apartment. Carmen is an excellent chef and describes herself as horrible with electronics, but she manages to figure out how to put together a coffee machiine in the epilogue all by herself.

Relationships

Helena

As kids, Carmen looked up to Helena as her older sister. However, when she moved out, they began to grow distant, until the only thing keeping them together was Helena lending Carmen money. However, after Helena sells her camera to pay Carmen's debts, Carmen and her reconcile and put their differences behind them. They end up working together at the cafe.

Maria

Carmen quickly clicks with Maria, quickly becoming close with her and asking her for favors. She's open with Maria, telling her about her relationship with Helena and confiding in her emotions. After Maria leaves Bellariva, Carmen offically thanks her for what she has done for her and Helena.
