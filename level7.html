Level 7 - Playing Up

Pre-Restoration

It's the day before the festival and Maria has been very busy. When Joseph calls her to the house with a job, Izzy diverts her attention first. She brings Maria to the playhouse in the garden and asks for top secret help with fixing her broken toy.

Restoration - Yokoi Pop'n'Play: Games Console

This level taks place on a blue table with doodles. The coffee cup in the corner leads to the menu.

  1. Remove the back of the top half of the toy, along with the wires and the circuit board.
  2. Add the new circuit board and add the chip with a red circle.
  3. Redo the two wires, connecting the one dot icon with the four corners icon and the three dots icon with the light icon.
  4. Remove the back of the bottom half of the toy, along with the wires and chip.
  5. Remove the circuit board and replace the broken button.
  6. Place the circuit board back and add a new chip.
  7. Add three wires, connecting the one dot icon to the four corners icon, the two dots icon to the music icon, and the three dots icon to the light icon.
  8. Add a wire to the top half of the toy, connecting the two dots icon to the music icon, and replace the backs of the toy.
  9. Insert the game and switch the toy on.
  10. Press a, then press the down key four times, the right key five times, and the up key seven times. Press a again once the bird is in the square.

Post-Restoration

As Izzy plays her game, Maria makes to leave. Izzy asks her to keep it from Joseph, because he'll think she broke it on purpose. Izzy tells Maria that she doesn't want to go to the festival, as she thinks that her dad doesn't see her unless it's for his work. After saying this, she disappears into the garden and Maria reflects on her relationship with her parents.
