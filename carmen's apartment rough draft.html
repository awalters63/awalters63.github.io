Carmen's Apartment

Description

The apartment that Carmen lives in is equal parts chaotic and charming. Despite calling it a mess and full of knick-knacks, Helena later ends up moving in with Carmen. Maria compliments the apartment, saying that it fits the vibe of a person like Carmen.
