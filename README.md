	<h2>Assemble with Care</h2>
	
	<p>Assemble with Care is an indie puzzle game, developed by <a href="https://ustwogames.co.uk/">ustwo games</a>. It was released on March 26th, 2020, to very positive reviews.</p>
	
	<h2>About This Game</h2>
	
	<p>"When Maria, a globe-trotting antique restorer, arrives in the sun-soaked town of Bellariva, she has no idea just how broken it will turn out to be. She wants nothing more than to help the town’s inhabitants save their most beloved possessions, but when it’s their personal lives that are starting to fracture, she’ll need to find a way to hold them together, one spare screw at a time." - <a href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/1202900/Assemble_with_Care/">Steam Library</a></p>

	<h2>Level 1 - Arrival</h2>
	<h3>Pre-Unpacking:</h3>
		<p><a href="#">Maria</a> is on the train to <a href="#">Bellariva</a>, excitedly looking forward to the chance to take a break from her year of traveling and restoring antiques. Before she can explore, however, she needs to unpack her suitcase.</p>
		
	<h3>Unpacking - Aventura Classic: Suitcase</h3>
		<p>This level takes place on the tiles of the <a href="#">hotel</a> floor. The coffee cup in the corner leads to the menu.</p>
		<ol>
			<li>Remove the toolbox and set it to the side. <i>(After this, the order of steps is up to the player.)</i></li>
			<li>Remove the overalls and set them to the side.</li>
			<li>Remove the sunglasses and set them to the side.</li>
			<li>Remove the hairbrush and set it to the side.</li>
			<li>Remove the earrings, one at a time, and set them to the side.</li>
			<li>Remove the passport and set it to the side.</li>
			<li>Remove the picture and set it to the side.</li>
			<li>Remove the camera and set it to the side.</li>
			<li>Remove the flyers and set them to the side.</li>
		</ol>
		
	<h3>Post-Unpacking:</h3>
		<p>Maria is in <a href="#">Bellariva</a> to visit their food festival, however she will need to do jobs in order to pay for it. After she is settled, she will pass out her flyers in order to find work. The player learns that Maria's parents owned an antique shop, and that is where she learned to make repairs and gained her love for antiques.</p>
</article>

	<h2>Level 2 - Lullaby</h2>
	<h3>Pre-Restoration:</h3>
		<p>It is <a href="#">Maria</a>'s first morning in <a href="#">Bellariva</a>. She wanders through the town, noticing the preparations in the <a href="#">Main Square</a>. <a href="#">Izzy</a> taps her on the shoulder and asks her to fix her cassette deck and tape.</p>
		
	<h3>Restoration - Minato: Cassette Recorder</h3>
		<p>This level takes place on the stones of the <a href="#">Main Square</a>. The coffee cup in the corner leads to the menu.</p>
		<ol>
			<li>Turn the cassette deck around and unscrew the battery cover.</li>
			<li>Remove the batteries and replace them.</li>
			<li>Replace the cover and screw it back in.</li>
			<li>Pick up the tape and use the pencil to rewind it.</li>
			<li>Insert the tape into the cassette desk and press play.</li>
		
	<h3>Post-Restoration:</h3>
		<p>Izzy tucks the tape deck away carefully and Maria asks who the singer was. Izzy tells her that it was her dead mom. Her friends call her away and Izzy pays Maria in a variety of knick-knacks, which Maria acceptingly writes her a receipt for. After Izzy leaves, Maria reflects on how broken she seems over her mom's death, something Maria can't fix.</p>
</article>

			<li>Reapply glue and add the part with the arm carrying a book.</li>

			<li>Reapply glue and add the head.</li>
		</ol>
		
	<h3>Post-Repair:</h3>
		<p>Joseph talks about his relationship with his father and sets the statue down next to a family portrait of him, his wife, and Izzy. He tells Maria that he has been working late in preparation for the festival, which prompts her to remember how her parents would find ways to be busy so they wouldn't have to talk about her leaving. She hopes that in addition to his work, Joseph is making time for Izzy.</p>

			<li>Unscrew the back of the sign and remove it.</li>
			<li>Apply glue and attach the button.</li>
			<li>Attach a wire from the button to the circuit board.</li>
			<li>Press the power button.</li>
			<li>Glue on the new circuit strip and add another wire, adjusting them so that they are all connected.</li>
			<li>Press the power button.</li>
			<li>Glue on the new circuit strip and add another wire, adjusting them so that they are all connected, and pick an icon to place on the front. <i>Although there are three icons to choose from, the game defaults to the ice cream in other images.</i></li>
			<li>Press the power button.</li>
			<li>Replace the back of the sign and screw it back in.</li>
		</ol>
		
	<h3>Post-Assembly:</h3>
		<p>Maria and Carmen mount the sign and Carmen reflects on when she first opened the cafe. Helena arrives and comments on how the cafe isn't open, putting Carmen down again. She moves inside the cafe and says that the first thing they'll be doing is taking down the sign.</p>

	<h2>Maria</h2>
	<h3>Physical Appearance</h3>
		<p>Maria has brown hair that she wears up in a bun, tied with a pink scrunchie. She wears pink eyeshadow with her brown eyes and pink earrings. The scrunchie and earrings can be found in <a href="#">Level 1</a>. She wears a pink shirt with white overalls and shoes, as well as a gold bracelet.<br><br>In the photo of Maria in <a href="#">Level 1</a>, she has longer hair that she wears down and blue overalls over a cream sweater.</p>
		
	<h3>Personality</h3>
		<p>Maria is a restorer by trade. She grew up in her parents' antique store and used to daydream about where each of the objects came from. She left her parents a year ago to travel and restore beloved possessions, a career that she loves. She is very helpful, often going the extra mile to help her clients.</p>
		
	<h3>Relationships</h3>
		<h4>Her Parents</h4>
			<p>When Maria told her parents that she wanted to leave in order to travel the world, they didn't want her to leave. She remembers them finding work to be busy with, so that they wouldn't have to talk to her about her leaving. The day she left, Maria stole a suitcase out of their store and hadn't called them since. However, with the lessons she's learned in <a href="#">Bellariva</a>, Maria finally feels ready to reach back out to them to apologize.</p>
			
		<h4><a href="#">Izzy</a></h4>
			<p>Izzy is the first person Maria meets in Bellariva. She fixes her tape deck and plays along when Izzy pays her in knick-knacks. 	<h3>Personality</h3>
		<p>As the mayor of Bellariva, Joseph is devoted to his work, often to the point of overworking. He had a bad habit of prioritizing work over everything else, including Izzy, but his priorities shifted after the festival.</p>
		
	<h3>Relationships</h3>
		<h4><a href="#">Izzy</a></h4>

		<h4><a href="#">His Wife</a></h4>

		<h4><a href="#">His Father</a></h4>
			<p>Joseph's father was a strict man, who had high expectations. Joseph didn't live up to his standards and he realized he was turning into his father when he spent almost all of his time on work.</p>

			<p>Joseph initially only meant to pay Maria for Izzy's repair, but she endears herself to him after repairing his statue. As he sees her more, he opens up more to her, talking about his wife and his grief. Maria is responsible for the shift in Joseph's attitude.</p>

Bellariva tradition is that <a href="#">Joseph</a>, as mayor, goes around to each stall and tastes their food. At the end of the festival, he crowns one stall as the "Best Food in Bellariva." In the food festival during the game, <a href="#">Carmen</a> wins this title. In addition to the food stalls, there is also a market held.</p>
