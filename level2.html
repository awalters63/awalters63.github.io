Level 2 - Lullaby

Pre-Restoration:

It is Maria's first morning in Bellariva. She wanders through the town, noticing the preparations in the Main Square. Izzy taps her on the shoulder and asks her to fix her cassette deck and tape.

Restoration - Minato: Cassette Recorder

This level takes place on the stones of the Main Square. The coffee cup in the corner leads to the menu.

  1. Turn the cassette deck around and unscrew the battery cover.
  2. Remove the batteries and replace them.
  3. Replace the cover and screw it back in.
  4. Pick up the tape and use the pencil to rewind it.
  5. Insert the tape into the cassette desk and press play.

Post-Restoration:

Izzy tucks the tape deck away carefully and Maria asks who the singer was. Izzy tells her that it was her dead mom. Her friends call her away and Izzy pays Maria in a variety of knick-knacks, which Maria acceptingly writes her a receipt for. After Izzy leaves, Maria reflects on how broken she seems over her mom's death, something Maria can't fix.
